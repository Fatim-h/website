Board of Directors


Mr. Iftikhar H. Shirazi
Chairman

Mr. Iftikhar H. Shirazi serves as the Chairman of the Board, bringing over 30 years of distinguished experience in corporate management, particularly within the financial and trading sectors. With a Bachelor of Science in Finance from Notre Dame De Namur University and an OPM from Harvard Business School, Mr. Shirazi has held significant roles at Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi, Yamaichi Securities, and Toyota Tsusho Corporation. Currently, Mr. Shirazi leads Shirazi Investments (Pvt) Limited, Shirazi Trading Company (Private) Limited, Atlas Foundation, and Atlas Information Technology Resource Centre. In addition, he is affiliated with the SAARC Chamber of Commerce & Industry and the Federation of Pakistan Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FPCCI), and has previously served on the Board of Governors of the British Overseas School Association and as Vice President of the Management Association of Pakistan.

Mr. Aamir H. Shirazi
Director

Mr. Aamir H. Shirazi holds the position of President at Atlas Group and has a notable background in leadership and management. A graduate of Claremont McKenna College and Harvard Business School's OPM program, Mr. Shirazi was the Chief Executive of Atlas Honda Limited for over a decade. His career also includes serving as a professional director on the Board of Lahore Stock Exchange and as Honorary Consul General of Japan in Lahore since 2002.

Mr. Frahim Ali Khan
Director

Mr. Frahim Ali Khan contributes over 50 years of experience in General Management, Finance, Investment, and Taxation to the Board. His educational credentials include degrees in Commerce and Law from Karachi University, and advanced management programs from Harvard University, Stanford University, and INSEAD University. Mr. Khan joined the Atlas Group in 1967 and has held various senior positions throughout his tenure.

Mr. Saquib H. Shirazi
Director

Mr. Saquib H. Shirazi, the Chief Executive Officer of Atlas Honda Limited, is a graduate of the Wharton School of Finance and Harvard Business School. His extensive experience includes previous board positions with Pakistan Petroleum Limited, National Refinery Limited, and Sui Southern Gas Company Limited. Mr. Shirazi currently chairs the Pakistan Business Council and holds memberships with the Prime Minister’s Business Council, Engineering Development Board, and Pakistan Cables’ Board. He also served as President of Harvard Business School’s Global Alumni Board from 2006-2008.

Mr. Ali H. Shirazi
Director

Mr. Ali H. Shirazi, Chief Executive Officer of Atlas Battery Limited, offers a background in finance and law. He earned his undergraduate degree in Political Science from Yale University and a Masters in Law from Bristol University. His career includes roles with the Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi and American Honda. Mr. Shirazi is involved with several organizations, including the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology, National Management Foundation, Cherat Packaging Limited, and Techlogix International Limited.
